How to Submit Homework

1) Log in to your account.
2) On the right side of the screen, review the "To Do" list. You will see links titled similar to  "Turn in Homework X" or "Homework X".
3) Click the appropriate link.
4) On the new page, click Submit assignment at the top of the page.
5) On the bottom of the page, click the "Choose file" and navigate to the file you want to submit.
6) If you have additional files, repeat step 5. (Submit both the program(s) and the console output).
7) Click the Submit button. 
The submitted files will upload and will appear on the top of the right side of the page.
